    • /diy/ Do-it-yourself

    /diy/ is the place to find guides, tutorials or help with how to create things, how to fix stuff, or how to do something without anyone but yourself and those instructions.

    What kind of threads go on here?

    Threads in /diy/ all are either asking how to do something such as make your own cellphone or how to set up your sattelite dish without having to pay extra to get someone else who has experience to do it, or has a usually in-depth guide to how to make such a thing or how to save on something many people pay for when they don't actually need to pay for it.
