    • /po/ Papercraft and Oragami

    The /po/ board is where you discuss and share various things made out of paper, usually this board focuses on oragami because of its popularity.

    What kind of threads go on here?

    Request threads for how to create certain things people want to create are common, and people usually create threads to specifically share templates and print-outs to people. There are also threads discussing specific challenges or tips and techniques for users.
