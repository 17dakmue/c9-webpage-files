    Who is this 4chan?

    4chan is a imageboard-type website infamous for doing bad things and slightly illegal activities, it's personally my favourite site because there is almost never a dull moment. *wink wonk* The website is in an "imageboard" format, where people create "threads" for various topics, to ask for help, or just for fun. People reply to the threads, most of the time with relevant content but some people deviate from the topic and post their own stuff; this could be to troll people, "hijack" a thread, or just someone being stupid (AKA voice their opinions).


    You never need to sign up for 4chan, everyone is anonymous and anyone can post whatever they want with only remote threat of accountability for what they have said. Everyone being anonymous also includes you cannot "message" other users or establish any kind of social relationship without one of you publicly displaying information. Being a social network, this is weird to a lot of people and even sociologists have spent time just attempting to figure out how it works.

    What does 4chan do?

Most of the time when they raid, it's generally because they don't like that group because they quarrel with 4chan because of the nature of the website.
