    • /g/ Technology General

    The /g/ board of 4chan is the place for talking about technology and generally things surrounding technology. But what really happens on /g/ is people being basement neckbeards and arguing over how people use their programs or how they buy pc's and their operating systems superiority. It's mostly on topic about tech but people will get in arguments a lot if some user is using X thing or does X instead of Y.

    What kind of threads go on here?

    Generally speaking, threads about technology are a very common occurance, few threads are off-topic. There is usually a Daily Programming thread where you post what you are working on, there is a Desktop thread where people post their "riced"(fancied up) desktops and complain about operating systems's, there is a Battlestation thread where people post about how their workstation/dekstop where their computer is and post pictures to compare them to others.
